Al Ameen College of Pharmacy

The Al Ameen College of Pharmacy is located in Bangalore, Karnataka, and was founded in 1983. Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Science (RGUHS), located in Bangalore, is associated with the college. Additionally, the Pharmacy Council of India (PCI) has authorized it.

Al Ameen College of Pharmacy provides top-notch instruction in the pharmacy sector via a variety of degrees, including PhD, Pharm D, M.Pharm, D.Pharm, and B.Pharm. The academic standing of the applicants in the previous qualifying test determines their admission to any of the college's UG and PG courses. The PhD admissions process begins with the personal interview and ends with academic merit. The candidates' performance in the personal interview determines the final choice.

Admission

Al-Ameen College of Pharmacy offers B.Phram, D.Pharm, M.Pharm, Pharm.D and Ph.D. courses.
Candidates who have passed 10+2 in science stream can apply for admissions to undergraduate courses.
Candidates must hold a Bachelor’s degree in Pharmacy for admission to postgraduate courses.
Admissions to UG and PG courses are merit-based while admissions to Ph.D. are based on the Personal Interview round by the college.

Al-Ameen College of Pharmacy D.Pharm, B.Pharm and Pharm.D Admission 2024

Candidates must have passed the two-year Pre-University Course (PUC) examination of the Karnataka PUC Board or an equivalent examination of any other approved board or university with at least 40% marks in any combination of PCM, PCB, PCMB, or PC with Computer Science, Electronics, Information Science, or Biotechnology in order to be considered for admission to the D.Pharm, B.Pharm, and Pharm.D. programs.

In addition to the second B.Pharma subjects, candidates who have passed the D.Pharma course approved by the Pharmacy Council of India will be admitted directly to the second B.Pharma course, 10% above the authorized intake. These candidates will be required to study statistics, computer science, and mathematics from the first B.Pharma course.

Al Ameen College of Pharmacy Facilities

  • Library / Information Centre :The college has an excellent library which acts as an information centre to students who seeks information. The central library provides its service to the user community through seven well equipped departmental libraries and two hostel libraries.
