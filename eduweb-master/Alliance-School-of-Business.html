Alliance School of Business

MBA admission is now being accepted at Alliance School of Business. Bengaluru has PGDM and MBA programs that are based on the Alliance Management Aptitude Test (AMAT) and personal interviews. Candidates, however, must possess a valid score from any national or state examination, such as the CAT, MAT, CMAT, NMAT, GMAT, KMAT, or XAT. Additionally, the Alliance School of Business in Bengaluru provides a Ph.D. in management, a Global MBA, a BBA (Hons. ), and a B.Com (Hons.).

AUMAT is used to determine admission to BBA (Hons. ), which is then followed by a personal interview. Admission to the Global MBA program is determined by the candidate's profile, which includes work experience and qualifying exam scores. For the Doctoral program, admission is determined by the Alliance Research Aptitude Test (ARAT), which is followed by an interview.

Alliance School of Business Admission 

At the Alliance School of Business, admission is determined by both entrance and merit. The AUSAT and Alliance-AMAT are administered by Alliance University to select candidates for Undergraduate and Graduate programs, respectively. National entrance tests such as MAT, CAT, CMAT, KMAT, and so on are also accepted by Alliance School of Business for admission to MBA programs. The school's best courses are the MBA, EPGDM, and BBA. Under Alliance University, the Alliance School of Business provides five degree programs at the undergraduate, graduate, and doctorate levels in a range of specializations. Alliance School of Business Admission Process 

The application procedure, selection round, cutoff, merit list, counseling, and additional procedures are all part of the admissions process. The Alliance School of Business admissions procedure can be found below:

STEP:1

The mode of application is online for all courses. Candidates need to pay an application fee to apply for admission. The application fee for BBA, BCom and MBA is INR 1,000 while, for PhD, it is INR 3,000.

Step 2: Selection Rounds

The selection rounds vary for UG and PG courses. Candidates need to qualify MAT, CAT, ALLIANCE-MAT, XAT, NMAT, CMAT, KMAT score, Oral Extempore Speech followed by PI for MBA course. For BCom and BBA, candidates need to qualify AUSAT followed by a personal interview round.

Step 3: Merit List & Counselling

As per the performance of the candidates in the selection rounds, a merit list is released by the school. Candidates with their names in the merit list are required to appear for the counselling round. They need to pay the fee to confirm their admission.

How to apply?

The mode of application is online for all courses. Candidates need to pay an application fee to apply for admission. Check below the steps to apply for Alliance School of Business admission:
