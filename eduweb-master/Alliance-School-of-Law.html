Alliance School of Law,Banglore

Alliance School of Law was established in 2010 and is affiliated with Alliance University, Bangalore. The college is approved by BCI and is a member of the LSAC Global Law Alliance. The other schools of study under Alliance University are Alliance College of Engineering and Design, Alliance Ascent College, Alliance School of Business, and Alliance School of Liberal arts. Alliance School of Law, Bangalore is ranked 11th in Law School for Outstanding Excellence CSR-GHRDC Law School Survey 2018 and awarded the Most Promising Law School by GHRDC Law School. Alliance University is ranked 25 by NIRF 2023 under the Law category
