Bhagawan Buddha Homeopathic Medical College and Hospital: 

The goal of the Buddha Education Society & Trust, which oversees the Bhagawan Buddha Homeopathic Medical College and Hospital, is to provide top-notch employment opportunities and medical education to students looking for professional programs that will ensure them fulfilling careers.

Every student at this college is provided with the foundation for successful medical performance. Students develop their ethical and professional behavior, respond to and value cultural diversity, and demonstrate their promise to society throughout the training process. This College has formed about 150 Doctors till date. The need for homoeopathy and homoeopathic medical education is growing, meeting the needs of the society's medical conditions. The teaching staff at the organization and all of the college's supporters are adamant about making the college the best homeopathic college in Karnataka.

Eligibility
1) A Candidate seeking admission to first BHMS Course, shall have passed two years Pre-University Examination Conducted by Department of Pre-University Education, Karnataka State with Physics, Chemistry and Biology as Optional Subjects with 50% marks in aggregate in PCB.

Or

2) Shall have Passed Intermediate Examination in Science of an Indian university / Board / Council or other recognized examining bodies with Physics, Chemistry and Biology, which shall include a practical test in these subjects .The candidate shall have passed subjects of Physics, Chemistry and Biology individually with 50% marks in aggregate in Physics, Chemistry and Biology.

Or

3) Shall have passed B.Sc. Examination of an Indian university ,Provided that he/she has passed the B.Sc. Examination with not less than two of the following subjects : Physics, Chemistry, Biology(Botany, Zoology) Provided the candidate has passed subjects of Physics, Chemistry and Biology Individually in the qualifying examinations in PUC or 10+2 equivalent.


Age : The candidate should have completed 17 Years of age on or before 31st day of December of the year of admission.
