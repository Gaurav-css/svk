CMC,Vellore

Founded in 1942, the Christian community runs Christian Medical College, Vellore, a private medical facility that also serves as a hospital. NIRF 2023 has CMC Vellore classified as the third best medical college. Two campuses make up CMC Vellore. The primary campus is located in Vellore, while the secondary campus is located 7 km distant in Bagayam City. In addition to 44 Allied Health Science courses and 52 fellowship courses, including courses in biostatistics, medical physics, bioengineering, hospital management, and many other subjects, CMC Vellore offers 57 post-graduate diploma and degree medical courses.

  • CMC Vellore offers UG, PG, and PhD courses in Medical, Allied Sciences, Nursing, and Physiotherapy.
  • BBA in Hospital Management course is offered for women candidates only for the 2023-2024 academic year.

BPT Specializations

Physical therapy for the spine: Orthopedic physiotherapy requires an understanding of the skeleton's anatomy, physiology, and pathology, as well as soft tissues. The treatment of diseases of the musculoskeletal system is the focus of orthopedics. Back and neck pain, hip fracture, osteoporosis, arthritis, and bursitis are some of the most common conditions that Orthopedics treat.

Physical therapy for the brain: It is a specialty that deals with treating people who have issues with their movement and other bodily functions as a result of malfunctions in the nervous and neuromuscular systems in their bodies. Problems with movement and other body functions result from any damage to the central nervous system, including the brain and spinal cord. Spinal cord injury, brain injury, stroke, and balance issues are among the neurological conditions that necessitate physiotherapy.

Physiatry for children: It is a specialization that focuses on a baby's growth and development, particularly its movement. Pediatricians strive to enhance the quality of life of children with neuromuscular conditions like autism and cerebral palsy.

Physiotherapy for the elderly: Disorders affecting older adults are the focus of this specialization. A physiotherapist who specializes in this field treats conditions like arthritis and osteoporosis in the elderly.
