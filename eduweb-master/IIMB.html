IIM Bangalore (IIMB)

Indian Institute of Management Bangalore (IIMB) is a leading graduate school of Management in Asia. IIMB has been ranked 2nd among the top Management institutes by NIRF 2023 and 31st in the Masters in Management category by the QS World University Ranking 2024. In addition, the institute has also been ranked 3rd by Business Today ranking 2023.Established in 1973, IIMB is famous for its excellent faculty members who have studied and taught at some of the best universities in India and abroad. The courses offered at IIM Bangalore include Certificate, Postgraduate, and PhD programmes in the Management stream. During IIM Bangalore placements 2023, a total of 606 offers were made to PGP and PGP BA. IIM Bangalore average package stood at INR 35.31 LPA. IIM Bangalore course admission is based on the entrance exams, namely CAT, GATE, etc. IIM Bangalore cutoff is based on scores obtained in the CAT exam, followed by WAT, GD and PI.

IIM Bangalore (IIMB) Course

Postgraduate, doctorate, and certificate programs in management are available at IIM Bangalore. Applications for the IIM Bangalore PGDM are accepted from applicants with a bachelor's degree from an accredited institution and a minimum cumulative GPA of 50%. Admission to the IIM Bangalore flagship PGDM program is handled through the CAT. The institute provides a one-year Executive MBA study in addition to PGDM. Applicants must have at least five years of work experience and a bachelor's degree from an accredited university in order to be considered for admission to the IIM Bangalore Executive MBA program.

IIM Bangalore Admission

Admission to the Indian Institute of Management Bangalore (IIMB) is highly competitive and follows a rigorous selection process. IIM Bangalore offers various programs such as the Post Graduate Program in Management (PGP), Fellow Program in Management (FPM), Executive Post Graduate Program (EPGP), and several executive education programs. The PGP is the flagship program and is the most sought-after.

Here is a general overview of the admission process for the PGP at IIM Bangalore:

  1. Eligibility Criteria:Candidates must have a bachelor's degree in any discipline with a minimum percentage of marks, usually around 50% or equivalent CGPA.For some programs, work experience may be preferred but is not always mandatory.
  2. Common Admission Test (CAT):CAT is the primary entrance exam for admission to IIMs, including IIMB.Candidates must take the CAT exam and obtain a valid score.
  3. Application Process:Candidates need to fill out the online application form on the official website during the application window.The application form typically includes personal details, academic history, work experience (if any), and other relevant information.
  4. Shortlisting for Personal Interview (PI) and Written Ability Test (WAT):Based on CAT scores and other criteria, candidates are shortlisted for the next stage, which involves a Personal Interview and a Written Ability Test.Shortlisting criteria may also include academic performance, work experience, diversity factors, etc.
  5. Interview and WAT:Shortlisted candidates are invited to participate in a Personal Interview and Written Ability Test.The WAT assesses a candidate's written communication skills, and the PI evaluates various aspects like personality, academic achievements, work experience, and general awareness.
  6. Final Selection:The final selection is based on a composite score that considers CAT scores, academic performance, work experience, diversity factors, and performance in the PI and WAT.
  7. Merit List:The institute prepares a merit list based on the composite scores, and candidates are offered admission accordingly.
  8. Admission Offer and Acceptance:Selected candidates receive admission offers, and they need to accept the offer within a specified period by paying the admission fee.

IIM Bangalore Placement

The placement committee at the Indian Institute of Management Bangalore offers good opportunities to the right candidates, resulting in consistently high placements for students. IIM Bangalore placement preparation is very well taken care of by highly trained and soft-skilled trainers. IIM Bangalore placement cell organises training activities for students such as seminars, interactive sessions, industry talks, group discussions with experts, and many other activities. IIM Bangalore placement cell handles students' placements' specific skill sets from the day of their programme and brings them into the right frame of work. The top recruiters visit the campus for recruitment drives that are conducted every year.

IIM Bangalore Hostels

Accommodation & Other Expenses

Accommodation:  Students visiting IIMB under the student exchange program during Term 5(Sep-Nov) may be provided accommodation in the hostel during the exchange period.  Students visiting in other terms, Term 4 or Term 6, may not have access to hostel rooms, in which case IIMB will assist in finding suitable accommodation. However, IIMB is not responsible for finding such accommodation. The costs of accommodation and meals must be borne by the students.
