Krupanidhi College of Pharmacy

Founded in 1985, Krupanidhi College of Pharmacy holds an NAAC A+ accreditation. Both the All India Council of Technical Education and the Pharmacy Council of India have given it their approval. Krupanidhi is associated with Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences, which is situated in Karnataka. It has one of the most cutting-edge infrastructures and some of the best practitioners in the field on its faculty. The twenty state-of-the-art labs of Krupanidhi College of Pharmacy are furnished with the newest technology and state-of-the-art facilities.

The college provides a number of pharmacy degrees, including UG-level B.Pharm and Pharm (D) courses and PG-level M.Pharm and Pharm (D) -PB courses. Additionally, it provides Ph.D. programs at the doctorate level and Diploma courses in D.Pharm courses. Admissions to the offered courses are determined by admission tests as well as merit. Online application mode is available. Top employers that come to the college include Himalaya, SPI Pharma, Cipla, Biocon, Kemwell, and Bio Plus.

Krupanidhi College of Admission

​The Krupanidhi College of Pharmacy (KCP) opened its doors in 1985. It is a pharmacy college with an NAAC "A" grade and is connected to Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences. Undergraduate B.Pharm and Pharm (D) courses are offered by Krupanidhi College of Pharmacy (KCP), while postgraduate M.Pharm and Pharm (D) -PB courses are available. Additionally, it provides a Ph.D. program as a doctorate degree and a Diploma course in D.Pharm.

  • ​Admission for the B.Pharm., Pharm (D) courses will be done based on PUC / 10 + 2 / PDC / Intermediate / Higher Sec./Vocational marks.
  • dmission to the M.Pharm., Pharm (D) - PB courses will be done on the marks obtained in B.Pharm. or relevant undergraduate degree courses from any recognised university.
  • Admission to the D.Pharm course will also be done on the basis of thePUC / 10 + 2 / PDC / Intermediate / Higher Sec./Vocational marks.
  • Admission to Ph.D courses will be done on the marks obtained in M.Pharm. degree courses from any recognised university.

Krupanidhi College of Pharmacy B.Pharm. Admission

The eligibility criteria to get admission to the B.Pharm course is that the candidates should have passed 10+2 examinations with Physics and Chemistry as compulsory subjects along with one of the following - Mathematics / Computer Science / Biotechnology / Biology. Obtained at least 40% marks (35% in case of candidate belonging to reserved category) in the above subjects taken together.

Candidates who have passed the D.Pharm course approved by the Pharmacy Council of India shall also be eligible for this course and can be admitted directly to II Year

KCP B.Pharm. Selection Criteria

  • Selection will be done based on 10+2 marks and KCET 2022 entrance exam scores.
  • KCP will release a merit list and based on the same admissions will be conducted in the B.Pharma program of KCP.
  • Take a printout of the form and attach it with supporting documents. The supporting documents to be taken are as follows:
    • Original & photocopies of Marksheets and certificate of 10th & 12th.
    • Passport-size photographs.
    • Category Certificate (original), if applicable
    • Domicile Certificate.
    • Rank Card of KCET 2022.
    • Anti-ragging affidavit signed by the candidate and the parents separately
    • Income Certificate, if applicable
  • All of the documents and application forms (with originals) are submitted to the college for verification.
  • After verifying the documents, The Principal or the Registrar shall ensure the acceptance of the candidate. The candidates will be provided provisional admission.

Krupanidhi College of Pharmacy Scholarship

The Krupanidhi College of Pharmacy provides a range of fee waiver programs for students who demonstrate exceptional academic performance or who require financial assistance. Students can apply for one of two scholarship kinds through the college's official website. The students can apply for scholarships on the college website https://pharmacy.krupanidhi.edu.in/scholarship. The first is the Sports Scholarship and the other is the Merit Scholarship Form, which must be filled out with the candidate's basic information, educational background, and academic standing. Based on the credibility of the students, the college offers them scholarships.

Krupanidhi College of Pharmacy Facilities

KCP is one of the colleges residing in the massive 11 acres of land of the Krupanidhi Group of Institutions located in Bengaluru, Karnataka. Krupanidhi Institutes provide free Wi-Fi access inside its campus to all students. The library and hostel facility is common for all the institutes on the campus. KCP provides the following facilities to its students.

  • Laboratories: KCP has high-tech laboratories set up with modern infrastructure; the college has eighteen of such labs. All the laboratory personnel or staff are well-trained and experts in their field. The list of the laboratories can be accessed under the Laboratories tab of this webpage https://pharmacy.krupanidhi.edu.in/campus
  • Hostel: The campus has hostels for girls and boys. The institute provides clean and well-furnished rooms with different occupancy requirements for the students and free Wi-Fi access. There is a continuous process of expanding the hostel to incorporate more students.
  • Center for Pharmaceutical Professional Advancement (CPPA): KCP offers this program intending to enhance the student’s academic skills, hone soft skills, and make them employable by the top recruiters. The program strives to bridge the gap between an education course and employment. The program focuses on improving the analytical and comprehension skills of the students.
  • Extracurricular activities: The institute has varied sports facilities for volleyball, basketball, badminton, football, and cricket. There is a separate basketball court for the students. Due to the active participation of students and faculty, there are many sports clubs for different activities. The college also has societies and clubs such as the NSS, IT club, music club, and cultural club. There is also a Women's Development cell.
