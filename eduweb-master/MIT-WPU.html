MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU)

popularly known as MIT-WPU Pune, is one of the leading private engineering and management institutions in India, located in Kothrud, Pune. MIT-WPU Pune is a part of the MIT Group of Institutions and has been recognized by UGC. MIT-WPU Pune is a premier institution of higher learning offering 150+ Programs at different levels across various streams including Management, Engineering, Design, Health Sciences, Liberal Arts, Law, and many others.
  1. MIT-WPU Pune offers various UG, PG, Diploma, and Ph.D. courses through its 12+ Schools and 30+ Departments.
  2. B.Tech, M.Tech, and MBA are the flagship courses offered by MIT-WPU Pune.
  3. MIT-WPU Pune offers courses such as BBA, B.Com, B.Des, BA, B.Sc, and BFA at the UG level.
  4. At the postgraduate level, the institute offers courses such as M.Sc, MCA, LLM, and M.Des.
  5. MIT-WPU Pune also offers Ph.D. in all major streams along with a few Diploma and Certificate courses.
  6. Admission to MIT-WPU Pune is based on National-Level entrance exams or MIT-WPU CET conducted by the institute itself.
  7. MIT-WPU Pune Fees for B.Tech range between INR 2,55,000 and INR 3,55,000 Per Annum depending on the specialization opted.
  8. MIT-WPU Pune MBA Fees is INR 4,55,000 Per Annum, while for M.Tech, the annual fee is INR 1,95,000.

MIT-WPU Pune Admission 2024

MIT-WPU Pune Admission to various UG, PG, Diploma, and Doctorate courses is entrance-based. Candidates will be selected for admission either through National Level Entrance Tests, Maharashtra CET, or MIT-WPU CET (Entrance Test). MIT-WPU Pune Admission is purely based on the rank secured by the candidate in the respective entrance exam followed by subsequent rounds of selection such as Personal Interaction(PI)/GD. Candidates seeking admission to MIT-WPU Pune need to apply online on its official website within the specified dates.

MIT-WPU Placements

MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU) has a good placement record with a high percentage of students being placed in top reputed national and multinational companies every year. MIT-WPU Pune Placements have concluded for Batch 2023. The highest package at MIT-WPU Pune Placements 2023 stood at INR 51.36 LPA while the average package stood at INR 7 LPA.
