PES University:

Known as PES Bangalore, (PES) University is a private university situated in Bangalore, Karnataka, that was founded in 1972. The PES Institute of Technology was the previous name of the university. In 2013, the institute changed its name. The Karnataka government and UGC both acknowledge PES University. PES Bangalore provides programs in engineering, law, commerce & management, architecture, design, science & humanities, and pharmaceutical sciences at the undergraduate, graduate, executive, and research levels. The IIRF has placed PES University in 77th place for the year 2023.

  • Undergraduate studies in B.Tech, BA, B.Arch, B.Com, BBA, B.Sc., B.Des, and other subjects are available at PES University.
  • PES Bangalore administers the PESSAT in order to grant admission to students for various programs.
  • PES University admits students to its PG programs, which include MBA, MCA, M.Tech, M.Com, and M.Pharm.
  • The results of national level entrance exams, including UCEED, PGCET, NATA, CAT, MAT, and others, are accepted by PES University.
  • PES Bangalore offers admission to interested applicants based on their performance in the KCET.

PES University Admission

PES University Bangalore admissions 2024 are based on merit in the PESSAT conducted by the institute. PES University also accepts other national-level entrance examination scores to offer admission to certain courses like, B.Tech, MBA, LLB, MCA, B.Des, M.Tech, PhD, and others. PES Bangalore conducts offline interviews for granting admission to some courses.

PES University Application Process 2024

The applicant has to follow the steps mentioned below in order to apply for PES University admission in 2024.

  • Visit the PEESAT registration website 
  • The candidate has to select the preferred course.
  • A new window will be displayed asking for the course type.
  • Choose the “PEESAT Only” option, in case you do not have any other valid entrance scores.
  • Fill out the application form by mentioning the required details like name, e-mail address, username, password etc.
  • Once the application form is submitted successfully, the candidate has to pay the fees.

PES University Facilities

PES University Bangalore is spread across 30 acre of land with state-of-the-art facilities and infrastructure. PES University Bangalore provides several facilities to undergraduate and postgraduate students like a sports complex, library, hostel, labs, and offers other facilities to students. The university has various clubs like Chef Club, Quiz Clubs, and others.

  • Classrooms: Well-equipped classrooms with modern teaching aids and technology to facilitate effective learning.
  • Laboratories: Specialized labs for various disciplines, including computer labs, science labs, and engineering labs, to provide hands-on experience to students.
  • Library: A comprehensive library with a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources to support research and academic needs.
  • Hostel Accommodation: On-campus hostels with facilities for students coming from different parts of the country or abroad.
  • Sports Facilities: Sports grounds, courts, and facilities for various sports and physical activities.
  • Auditoriums: Large auditoriums for hosting events, seminars, conferences, and cultural activities.
  • Cafeteria: Dining facilities providing a variety of food options for students and staff.
  • Medical Facilities: Health services and medical facilities for students' well-being.
  • Transportation: Bus or transportation services for commuting to and from the campus.
  • Wi-Fi and IT Infrastructure: Internet connectivity and IT infrastructure to support research, online learning, and communication.
  • Recreation Areas: Spaces for students to relax and engage in recreational activities.
  • Research Centers: Facilities dedicated to research in various disciplines.

Hostels

PES University hostels are available for boys and girls separately. The hotels have well-furnished rooms, laundry facilities, a library, and a mess which provide veg and non-veg food options.
