PGIMER Chandigarh

Located in Chandigarh, India, the Postgraduate Institute of Medical Education & Research (PGIMER) is a public university dedicated to postgraduate medical research. The Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Government of India, has designated PGIMER Chandigarh as a Center of Excellence and has designated it as a deemed university. It provides training, research, and instructional opportunities in all specializations, super specialties, and subspecialties of medicine. The Ministry of Education has classified PGIMER Chandigarh as the second-best medical institute in the NIRF Ranking 2023. It is one of the most well-liked postgraduate medical schools and is recognized as an Institute of National Importance.

  • PGIMER offers more than 50 postgraduate medical and paramedical programs.
  • The institute also offers Ph.D. and Post-Doctoral degrees in medical sciences.
  • Admissions to PGIMER Chandigarh are entrance-based followed by the respective counseling.
  •  PGIMER accepts INI CET for admission to MD, MS, D.M, M.Ch, and MDS courses.
  • PGIMER Chandigarh has a 277-Acre Campus with all the necessary and modern amenities.

The median package during PGIMER Placements was INR 17 LPA for the PG (3-Year) Batch and INR 8 LPA for the PG (2-Year) Batch, per the PGIMER Chandigarh NIRF Report 2023. The institute's 2022 Placement Drive places 205 students in total. Apollo Hospital, Fortis Hospital, Manipal Hospital, AIIMS, and other top hospitals are among the recruiters that frequent PGIMER Placements.

PGIMER Chandigarh Admission

The application period for PGIMER's 2024 PG, PhD, and post-doctoral programs is currently open. The public postgraduate university PGIMER Chandigarh has been named an Institute of National Importance and a Center of Excellence in medical research. The majority of admissions to PG and PhD programs in medicine are made by PGIMER Chandigarh. More than fifty postgraduate medical and paramedical programs, including the MD, MS, DM, M.Ch, and MPH, are available. A few UG programmes, including Post Basic B.Sc. Nursing and B.Sc. Nursing, are also offered by the institute.

At PGIMER Chandigarh, admission to postgraduate programs like MD, MS, M.Ch, and DM is determined by the results of the INI CET, which is followed by counseling. To get admitted to further undergraduate, graduate, and doctorate programs, PGIMER Chandigarh holds its own entrance exam. Candidates must first apply for the INI CET in order to be considered for admission to PGIMER Chandigarh's MD, MS, MDS, DM, and M.Ch programs. After that, they must show up for the counseling procedure to guarantee a spot at the university. Candidates must complete the application on the PGIMER official website in order to be considered for the remaining courses.

PGIMER Chandigarh UG Eligibility Criteria

  • Candidates must have passed 10+2 with 50% marks aggregate in Physics, Chemistry, English, and Biology/ Biotechnology from a recognized board.
  • For SC, ST, and other reserved categories, the minimum required percentage marks is 45% in aggregate.
  • The age limit for PIGMER UG Admissions is between 17-25 years. 
  • For BSc Nursing (Post Basic), the candidate must have completed 10+2 along with 50% marks in General Nursing and Midwifery and must be a registered nurse or midwife.

PGIMER Chandigarh Application Process 2024

PGIMER Chandigarh conducts admissions to various courses such as MD, MS, DM, and M.Ch twice a year in two sessions- January Session and July Session. The institute offers admissions to various PG courses on the basis of rank obtained by candidates in the INI CET (Institute of National Importance Combined Entrance Test).​ The candidates who clear the exam have to then appear for INI CET Counseling to secure a seat at PGIMER Chandigarh.

PGIMER Chandigarh Facilities

PGIMER Chandigarh has a vast campus and infrastructure providing all basic and extra-curricular amenities to its students. PGIMER Chandigarh offers the following facilities to its students: 

Library

  • PGIMER's Dr. Tulsi Das Library was founded in 1962.
  • It has 45,696 books, 57,610 bound journals, and 414 international and 96 national journals.
  • Currently, the library houses 4,851 theses from many fields.
  • Science Direct, MD consult, Wiley-Blackwell, Oxford Journals, and 494 online full-text journals are among the online databases subscribed to by the library.

Hostel

  • PGIMER Chandigarh also offers hostel accommodation to admitted students.
  • The institute offers partially furnished hostel accommodation within the campus of the Institute.
  • PGIMER doesn’t offer accommodation to Sponsored/Deputed/Foreign national candidates.
  • Candidates need to pay INR 5,000 as security money that is refundable.
  • PGIMER hostel are equipped with all basic amenities along with Mess/Canteen facilities.
other: GYM , Swimming Pool , Sports ,cultural Fest
