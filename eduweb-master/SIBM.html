SIBM Bangalore

One of the constituent schools of Symbiosis International University (Deemed To Be) is Symbiosis Institute of Business Management (SIBM), located in Bangalore. The institute is located on Hosur Road in Bangalore and was founded in 2008. The SIBM Institute offers MBA and Executive MBA programs, with the following specialties available: Family Managed Business & Entrepreneurship, Quantitative Finance, Marketing, Operations, and HR. SIBM Bangalore Admission

Postgraduate degree degrees in Management are available at the Symbiosis Institute of Business Management Bangalore (SIBM Bangalore). The institution provides working individuals with an Executive MBA in addition to full-time programs. Online applications are accepted at SIBM Bangalore. Admission to different courses is determined by merit or entrance exams.

It is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) as a component of Symbiosis International Deemed-to-be University. The National Assessment of Accreditation Council (NAAC) has awarded the institute a 'A' grade for accreditation. Reputable firms such as Accenture, Amazon, Capgemini, Credit Suisse, Dell, Deloitte, Genpact, Infosys, Maruti Suzuki India Limited, MTR Foods, Tata Motors, and Thoucentric place deserving students on campus. AB InBev, Bisleri, Cadila Pharmaceuticals, Reliance Broadcast Networks, Thoughtworks, and DHL are a few more employers who recruit.

SIBM Bangalore Admission 2024: How to Apply?

The following is a step-by-step application process for SIBM Bangalore:

  1. Visit SIBM Bangalore's official website.
  2. To register for the application process, select "Register" after selecting "Apply."
  3. Fill up the application and send it in with the necessary information.
  4. An OTP will be sent to the registered email address and mobile number for candidates.
  5. Fill out the SNAP registration form and enter the OTP.
  6. Following a successful registration, submit an application and pay the Rs 1,950 SNAP exam cost. 
  7. Enter your SNAP ID and password to register on the Institute's website.
  8. After completing all the necessary fields, submit the form.
  9. Pay the 1,000 INR application fee.

Documents required:

The list of documents required at the time of application is given below:

  • Class 10 and Class 12 marksheets
  • Bachelor's degree certificate
  • Master's degree certificate

SIBM Bangalore ​Scholarship

Symbiosis Institute of Business Management offers scholarships to deserving and meritorious students enrolled in postgraduate programs. Under this scheme, the institute will provide 50% of the tuition fee of the program in the form of a fee waiver. To be eligible for this, a student must maintain a minimum GPA of 7.50. The award of merit scholarship for PG programs shall be announced every year by the University.
