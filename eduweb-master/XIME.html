XIME Bangalore:

The private Xavier Institute of Management and Entrepreneurship was founded in 1991. Three campuses—one each in Bangalore, Kochi, and Chennai—are home to XIME. The AICTE has authorized XIME Bangalore. Additionally, the institute belongs to AIU.

At the postgraduate level, XIME Bangalore offers PGDM and PGDM BA.Marketing, finance, human resources, operations & IT, and analytics are among the specializations. Additionally, Infosys, KPMG, Accenture, HCL, EY, and other major recruiters were among those that visited Xavier Institute of Management and Entrepreneurship in 2023. XIME Bangalore Admission

The flagship 2-year PGDM program at XIME Bangalore is available in six specialties. With an enrollment of 180 students for the PGDM program and 60 students for the PGDM BA program, the curriculum is comparable to an MBA degree. Admission to XIME Bangalore for 2023 will open soon. Based on past student reviews found on collegedunia.com, individuals who score in the 90th percentile or higher on the entrance exam have a strong probability of being chosen.

Candidates will be chosen for the PGDM program at XIME Bangalore based on their overall performance in the group discussion, personal interview, extracurricular activities, academic background, and diversity assessments. The following lists the weights of the different criteria:

  • Academic Performance- 20%
  • Entrance Exam Score- 35%
  • Group Discussion & Personal Interview- 35%
  • Sports, Extra-Curricular Activities, Academic Diversity and Gender Diversity- 10%

In addition to the PGDM, XIME Bangalore accepts students into the University of Mysore's PhD program. Two scholars have successfully completed their Pre-Thesis Colloquium, and thirteen scholars are now pursuing PhD degrees through the XIME Research Center. The Entrance Exam, which is followed by the PI, is the basis for selecting candidates for the PhD. To be eligible for the next round of admissions, students must receive at least 55 points on the entrance exam. For final admission, XIME Bangalore also takes previous academic standing and employment experience (if any) into account. Examine the XIME Fees & Courses.

XIME Bangalore Scholorships

XIME maintains its recognition and encouragement of its students' exceptional performance throughout all three of its campuses, as well as providing support to those students who require financial aid. The amount allotted for student recognition in the FY 2022–2023 budget was much more than INR 50 lakhs. The institute suggested raising it even further this year. Details about the scholarship are provided below:

  • Prof. J Philip Scholarship to a Meritorious Student at the Bangalore Campus. Prof. Philip is the Principal Founder of XIME and currently the Chairman of XIME Bangalore.
  • Fr. Dr. E. Abraham S.J. Scholarship at XIME Bangalore in memory of one of the XIME founders and the longest-serving Director of XLRI.
  • Prof. J.D. Cherayil Merit Scholarship at XIME Bangalore in memory of the late Prof. Cherayil, a well-respected Senior Professor of IISc and a co-founder of XIME. 
  • Sarosh J Ghandy Scholarship to a Meritorious Student at XIME Bangalore. Mr. Ghandy was the Chairman of XIME during 2000- 2010.
  • XIME President Scholarship is offered at each of the campuses. The scholarship amounts vary from INR 1.5 to 6.0 lakhs.
