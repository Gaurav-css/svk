BIT Mesra has achieved the 53rd rank under engineering, the 20th rank under architecture, and the 77th rank under management category by NIRF Ranking 2023. Birla Institute of Technology, Ranchi was established in 1955 and founded by the industrialist Birla family. In 1972, it received the status of autonomy. BIT Mesra is a UGC-approved deemed university. With 17 departments, BIT Mesra offers undergraduate, graduate, and doctorate programs in the fields of engineering, science, management, pharmaceutical sciences, and hotel management.

BTech is the flagship program of BIT Mesra. Admission to the course is based on JEE Main rank. BIT Mesra offers 10 specializations in the BTech course. The total cost of the 4-year program is INR 13.5 lakhs. In 2023, INR 51 LPA and INR 11.57 LPA were the highest and median packages for BIT Mesra. Furthermore, a total of 982 job offers were extended to BIT Mesra placements in 2023. Among the primary recruiters were Adrosonic, Wipro, TCS, and Infosys. BIT has off-campus locations in a number of cities, like Allahabad, Noida, Jaipur, Lalpur, Deoghar, Patna, and Kolkata.

BIT Mesra Admission 2024

Birla Institute of Technology (BIT) Mesra offers a list of courses at the undergraduate and postgraduate and doctoral levels. Some of the popular courses of the institute are BTech, MBA, MCA, MSc, etc. BIT Mesra admission process for most of the courses is entrance-based. The technology institute accepts scores of national-level exams like JEE Main, JEE Advanced, GATE, CAT, NEET-UG, etc. Candidates can apply by submitting their application online for admission to BIT Mesra in the desired course.

BIT Mesra BTech & BE Eligibility Criteria
To be eligible for admission in B.E/B.Tech/B.Arch at BIT Mesra, a candidate should fulfill the minimum requirements. This includes the following:

  • Must have passed 10+2 or equivalent with English, Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and a minimum aggregate of 60%.
  • Must have scored a minimum rank in JEE Main exam as per the institute requirements.

BIT Mesra BTech & BE Selection Criteria

The selection at the BIT Mesra is solely based on the candidate’s JEE Main cutoff and the marks obtained in the class 10th and 12th. Counseling for the seats is done as per the JoSAA/CSAB norms.

For NRI/FS candidates, selection will be based on the marks obtained in Class 12 or equivalent.

BIT Mesra BTech & BE Application Process

  • Visit the official website of BIT Mesra to apply for the program.
  • Click on “Click Here To Apply Online” under the respective course.
  • Register using your email id and log in with the username and password.
  • Fill in the necessary details like personal information and academic details.
  • Fill in your JEE Main rank.
  • Pay the application fee and submit the form.

BIT Mesra Scholarships

BIT Mesra Scholarship worth nearly 2.25 crores is available for meritorious students of BIT. BIT Scholarships for meritorious students admitted in the B.Tech. and B.Arch Programs. Each year, the Welfare Department of the Government of Jharkhand awards scholarships to students who fall within the ST, PT, SC, and OBC categories in accordance with government regulations. Students must meet the eligibility requirements as announced by the Welfare Department, Government of Jharkhand, in order to be eligible for the award.

How to avail of BIT Mesra Scholarships?

The applications for all the below-listed scholarships will be filled after BIT Mesra sends a scholarship notification via email to all the students.
The scholarships offered by BIT Mesra are listed below:

The "BIT G. P. Birla Scholarship" program has been established to reward deserving students and make sure that they are not prevented from pursuing a BIT degree due to financial constraints. The entire sum of the scholarships will be Rs 2.10 crores annually. The eligibility criteria will be revised at the Institute's discretion to limit the number of students who will be granted scholarships so that the total amount of scholarships granted in a year does not exceed Rs 2.10 cr if the total number of students becoming eligible for the scholarships in accordance with the criteria as specified below exceeds the numbers who can be accommodated within the total yearly sum of Rs 2.10 crores for scholarships. The following categories of BIT GP Birla Scholarship/ Assistantship will be available to the students of all campuses:

(A) BIT GP Birla Scholarship: BIT GP Birla Scholarship will be provided to meritorious students solely based on their academic performance in MO 2021 without any consideration of their family income.
(B) BIT GP Birla Scholarship for B.Tech. and B.Arch. based on AIR in JEE (Main): Candidates who have secured prescribed All India Ranks in JEE (Main) are eligible to receive the scholarship under this category.
(C) BIT GP Birla Scholarship for M.Tech. based on GATE Percentile: This scholarship is available for those candidates who have scored 90 percentile or more in GATE and are eligible to receive this scholarship in their first semester.
(D) BIT GP Birla Scholarship for B.Tech. under Immersive Summer Research Experience (ISRE) at Reputed Foreign Universities: This scholarship will be provided to those students of B.Tech. programs who are selected for the Immersive Summer Research Experience (ISRE) at reputed foreign Universities.
(E) BIT GP Birla Assistantship BIT GP Birla Assistantship: This scholarship will be provided to needy meritorious students based on merit cum means. The academic performance in MO 2021 and the family income will be considered for this category.
(F) BIT GP Birla Assistantship on Compassionate Grounds: A very limited number of assistantships may be provided to students on compassionate grounds.
(G) BIT GP Birla Assistantship for Staff Wards: BIT GP Birla Assistantship will be provided to the staff wards who are studying in various programs at BIT Mesra including its Off Campuses.
