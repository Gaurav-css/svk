School of Commerce and Management, Christ University Admission

Founded in 1994, Christ University, Institute of Management is a private institution with its headquarters located in Bangalore. The National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) has granted the Institute, which is affiliated with Christ University, accreditation. Currently, the MBA program at Christ University's Institute of Management provides a number of specialties, such as business analytics, marketing, and lean operations and systems.

  • Candidates applying in MBA course must have passed graduation with at least 50% marks in aggregate.
  • Students appearing for final year examination of their Bachelor’s program may also apply for MBA program.
  • Candidates must have possess valid CAT/ GMAT/ MAT/ XAT/ CMAT/ATMA scores are eligible for admission.
  • Application forms along with the fee can be submitted online through the official website.
  • Upon successful submission, printout of the application should be sent to the institute along with the requisite fee and required documents.

Christ University, Institute Of Management How to Apply?

Christ University, Institute of Management accepts applications both offline and online. But in order to be considered for admission to the MBA program, applicants must register on the official website for the appropriate entrance exam.

  • To fill and submit application using online mode, candidate should register through the official website.
  • Upon registration, candidates should proceed to click on the “Admissions”. On the confirmation page, click on ‘Applications’ and then the preferred programme.
  • Fill-in all the required details Scan and upload recent passport size photograph (3.5 cms x 4.5 cms formal dress with white background) and click “Submit”.
  • Application registration fee may be submitted through Credit / Debit Card / Net banking facility (for online applicants only)
  • Choose the payment option and fill-in the required details. The application will be submitted online after payment of application processing fee.
  • After the payment is successful, the candidate will receive the application number with the option to print your application form.
  • Print and sign the application form. Keep a copy of the application form for further reference and to produce at the time of selection process.
