CMRIT

Established in 2000, the CMR Institute of Technology is recognised by the UGC and AICTE and has been accredited by NAAC with an ‘A+’ Grade. It is also affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University. The Institute offers 20+ UG, PG and doctoral programmes in various disciplines such as Engineering and Technology, Management and Computer Applications across 15+ departments. CMR Institute of Technology has signed MoUs with industrial organisations for research and development, consultancy and mentoring such as Infosys, IBM, and National Research Development Corporation, among others.

CMRIT offers a wide variety of courses for Undergraduate and Postgraduate applicants in the fields of Engineering, Management, and Science, admission to which is both merit and entrance-based. Admission to CMRIT for the various academic programs is based on Karnataka CET/COMED-K/JEE for the undergraduate programs, admissions to postgraduate programs are based on performances in PGCET/GATE/CMAT/CAT.

CMRIT Bangalore Placement

CMR Institute of Technology (CMRIT) concluded the 2022 recruitment process with 714 placement offers. A total of 150 companies visited the institute campus during the CMRIT placements 2022. The CMRIT highest package stood at INR 26.50 LPA and the average package offered was INR 6.35 LPA. Further, a large share of offers were of packages between INR 4 LPA and INR 7 LPA.
