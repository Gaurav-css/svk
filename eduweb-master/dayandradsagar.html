Dayananda Sagar University

In 2014, Dayananda Sagar University was founded in accordance with the Karnataka State Act. The university began as a division of Dayananda Sagar Institutions, which the late Shri Dayananda Sagar established in the 1960s. The concept of providing students with a high-quality higher education was the original one. Its two campuses provide students with a variety of programs. The DSU City Innovation Campus, situated at Kudlu Gate, is another name for the main campus, which is also referred to as the Harohalli Campus.

Numerous programs in the fields of health science, pharmacy, nursing, engineering, etc. are available at DSU Bangalore. By providing high-quality instruction and skills, it seeks to change and inspire its students to become responsible and creative citizens of the nation.

Admission

Dayananda Sagar College of Pharmacy (DSCP), located in Bangalore, Karnataka, was founded in 1991. The institution, which is associated with Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences in Bangalore, provides a 6-year Doctor of Pharmacy (Pharma D.), a 4-year Bachelor of Pharmacy (B. Pharma), and a 2-year Master of Pharmacy program.

  • The application process at Dayananda Sagar College of Pharmacy is done offline.
  • The KCET score determines whether applicants go to Pharma D and Pharma B.
  • Candidates for the D. Pharma program must have completed the PUC II/ 10+2/equivalent exam with PCM/PCB or a Diploma in Pharmacy.
  • The candidate must have received at least 45% of the possible points in PUC II, 10+2, or the Pharmacy Diploma in order to be admitted to the B. Pharma program.
  • The KCET score determines whether applicants go to Pharma D and Pharma B.
  • Candidates must have earned a minimum of 55% in their pharmacy bachelor's degree in order to be admitted to the M. Pharma program.
  • A PGCET exam appearance is essential for admission to the M.Pharm program.

DSCP Bangalore Pharma D. Admission 2023

Dayananda Sagar College of Pharmacy offers a doctoral degree in Pharmacy of 6 years duration. In order to be eligible for admission to the degree, a candidate must have completed PUC II, 10+2, or a Diploma in Pharmacy with PCB and PCM as required disciplines.

Course Name Eligibility Criteria Selection Procedure
Pharma D. Passed PUC II/ 10+2 with PCM/ PCB or a Diploma in Pharmacy. KCET

DSCP Bangalore B. Pharma Admission 

Dayananda Sagar College of Pharmacy offers B. Pharma to candidates who have passed PUC II/ 10+2 with minimum 45% marks in PCM/ Biology/ Comp. Science/ Electronics.

Candidates with a Diploma in Pharmacy with 45% marks are also eligible to apply.

Course Name Eligibility Criteria Selection Procedure
B. Pharma Minimum 45% marks in PUC II/ 10+2/ D. Pharma with PCM/ PCB. KCET

DSCP Bangalore M. Pharma Admission 2023

Dayananda Sagar College of Pharmacy offers M. Pharma under 4 disciplines. The criterion to take admission in M. Pharma needs the candidates to have passed a bachelor’s degree in pharmacy with 55% marks.

Selection of the candidates is based on Merit. However, the candidates can appear for PGCET  to get preference during admission process.

Course Name Specialization Eligibility Criteria Selection Procedure
M. Pharma Pharmaceutics, Pharmaceutical Chemistry, Pharmacology, Pharmaceutical Analysis A graduate degree in Pharmacy with 55% marks. Merit/ PGCET based

DSCP Bangalore Application Process

Candidates seeking admission to Dayananda Sagar College of Pharmacy can register themselves online for KCET/PGCET through the respective official website. The application process is done offline by the college.

  • Candidate can obtain the prospectus and application form available at the college admission office by paying a nominal application fee.
  • Candidate should fill the application form and affix the recent passport-sized photograph
  • Candidate should attach the following required documents along with the completed applications:SLSC/ 10th
  • mark sheet and passing certificate
  • PUC II/ 10+2-mark sheet and passing certificate
  • Diploma in Pharma – mark sheet and passing certificate
  • Degree Mark Sheet (for M. Pharma)
  • Migration Certificate
  • Conduct / character certificate
  • KCET/ PGCET Registration Card
  • KCET/ PGCET Admit Card and fee receipt
  • KCET/ PGCET Score Card
  • Caste Certificate (SC/ ST/ OBC), if applicable
  • Next, the candidate should submit the application form in the college admission office
