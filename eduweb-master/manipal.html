Manipal Institute of Engineering and Technology (MIT):

The first engineering institution in India to be entirely self-financed is Manipal Institute of Technology (MIT), located in Manipal. According to NIRF 2022, the institute is ranked 55th out of the best engineering colleges in India. Currently, Manipal Institute of Technology offers three undergraduate and graduate programs: B.Tech, M.Tech, and M.C.A. The B.Tech program is the school's flagship subject, and admissions are determined by a combination of counseling and MET test results.

Because it offers its students good work options, MIT Manipal is also well-known for its placements. The maximum package offered in the most recent placement drive of 2023 was INR 54.75 LPA, while the average package was INR 14.10 LPA. Among the major employers at the Manipal Institute of Technology are Accenture, Capgemini, Cleartax, Dell, Deloitte, Goldman Sachs, KPMG, HPE, L&T, MyGate, and TCS.

Manipal institute of technology Admission 2024

Admissions for all courses at Manipal Institute of Technology are now open for the 2024 academic year. The institute provides courses at the UG and PG levels in the following subjects: science, engineering, information technology, and business and management studies. The Manipal Institute of Technology has different admissions procedures depending on the required course. The Manipal Institute of Technology Entrance Exam is required of applicants seeking admission to the BTech and MTech programs. The MCA course's selection criteria are merit-based other from this.
