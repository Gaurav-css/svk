M. S. Ramaiah College of Law Bangalore:

S. Ramaiah College of Law (MSRCL) was established in 1995 and is affiliated to the Karnataka State Law University. It has been recognized by the Bar Council of India and UGC. The institute offers courses like BA LLB, BBA LLB, B. Com. LLB, post-graduate, and certificate courses in various specializations of Law. For the UG courses, students are admitted through the CLAT examination.

M.S. Ramaiah College of Law offers a range of 3 UG courses. These courses are aimed to provide students with the necessary skills and knowledge in their field of interest. Candidate can pursue in multiple range of courses like Certificate, B.A. LL.B., BBA LL.B., B.Com LL.B, PG Diploma, LL.M. with the stream of Law. From introductory courses to more advanced topics, there are a variety of courses to choose from. Students who join M.S. Ramaiah College of Law stand to gain professional level of expertize and proficiency through its highly trained & experienced faculty in the studies of Human Rights & International Humanitarian Law, Business Law. Courses offered by institute are well recognized and acknowlegded by approving body like BCI.
