Pandit Deendayal Energy University (PDEU) :

GERMI established Pandit Deendayal Energy institution (PDEU), a private institution, on April 4, 2007. Pandit Deendayal Petroleum University (PDPU) was its previous moniker. It is situated in Gujarat, India's Gandhi Nagar. The University Grants Commission (UGC) and the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) have both approved and recognized PDEU. It is a renowned university that provides excellent facilities and a high standard of instruction. Its state-of-the-art audiovisual classrooms enable students to understand the material with ease. Under the following schools, PDEU provides a broad range of undergraduate, graduate, and research courses.

It is the only university in Gujarat, granted graded autonomy by UGC. NAAC accredited PDEU with an ‘A’ grade & CGPA of 3.39 out of 4.0. PDEU has also been recognized by the Scientific and Industrial Research Organization, Govt. Of India. In 2023, it obtained the 94th NIRF rank in the management category.

Pandit Deendayal Energy University (PDEU) has been established as a Private University through the State Act enacted on 4th April 2007 to create a World Class University in energy education. PDEU operates through the following schools:

  • School of Petroleum Engineering
  • School of Petroleum Technology
  • School of Technology
  • School of Liberal Studies

PDEU Admission 2024

Through the State Act, GERMI created Pandit Deendayal Energy University (PDEU) as a Private University. Pandit Deendayal Petroleum University (PDPU) was its previous name. The university is situated in Gandhinagar City's Raisan village. The University has membership in the Association of Indian Universities (AIU) and is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC). .

PDEU has four schools, located on the same campus. The schools include the School of Petroleum Technology, School of Technology, School of Petroleum Management, and School of Liberal Studies. The university offers various courses at undergraduate, postgraduate and research level.

Admission in B.Tech and M.Tech is done through JEE Mains and GATE respectively. Candidates seeking admission in MBA will have to appear in CAT/XAT/NMAT. Interested candidates need to note that registration for NMAT and XAT has started and will end on November 10 and November 30 respectively . Admission to M.Sc. is based on Written Test and PI conducted by the university itself.

PDEU facilities

PDEU has a large campus spanning across 100 acres of lush green and serene environment. The university maintains its high reputation by providing several facilities and amenities to students all over its campus. These include state of the art infrastructure, accommodation and recreational facilities. Some of the said provisions offered by the university are as follows:

  • Infrastructure: The campus of PDEU consists of 21 Buildings, 57 Lecture Halls, 43 Laboratories/Workshops and 1,320 units of Laboratory Equipment. The infrastructure featured state of the art Labs, equipped with the latest technology, simulation models. IT systems are equipped with the latest hardware & software and Wi-fi connectivity
  • Library: The university has a well-stocked library with a large collection of 57,550 Books + 13,100 E-books, 150 Print Journals/Periodicals & Magazines + 5500 e-journals, 5,690 Digital Material (CDs, DVDs), 42 Daily National & International News-Papers, Full Access to Scopus & Web of Science.
  • Sports: The institute offers sports and recreational facilities to students on the campus. There are also 3 fully equipped gymnasiums.
  • Hostel: Hostel facilities are available for both male and female students. There are a total of 1,042 Hostel Rooms with a residential capacity of 2,680 students.
  • Cafeteria : The university houses a clean and hygenic cafeteria that provides students with a variety of healthy and delicious food items. There are four Dining Halls with 2,000 people Sitting Capacity
