Siddaganga Institute of Technology (SIT)

Siddaganga Institute of Technology, popularly known as SIT, was established in 1963. It was established by Sree Sree Shivakumara Swamiji under Sree Siddaganga Education Society, and is located in Tumkur, Karnataka. It is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University and accredited by the NAAC with “A++” grade. Siddaganga Institute of Technology offers 12 undergraduate,13 postgraduate and 16 PhD programmes. Some of the popular courses are B.E. / B.Tech, MBA/PGDM, MCA, M.E./M.Tech, B.Arch.

Siddaganga Institute of Technology holds 101 Rank in 2023 as per NIRF MHRD, Govt. of India . Admissions at Siddaganga Institute of Technology for its various courses are done on the basis of entrance exams after which it is followed by a counseling process. Exams accepted by the Institute are KCET, COMEDK, JEE Main

Siddaganga Institute of Technology Placements

Siddaganga Institute of Technology is the first institution among the affiliated colleges of the Visvesvaraya Technological University to establish a full-fledged Training and Placement Department.
Siddaganga Institute of Technology placement drive for 2023 is ongoing. The highest salary package stood at INR 46.54 LPA whereas for the average package stood at INR 13.75 LPA. Around 70 students received salary packages of INR 10 LPA as of now. This year placements and packages are much better than the previous year.

Siddaganga Institute of Technology Scholarship

  1. Sri Sripathi K.A. Memorial Scholarship:
  • The student must have secured his/her admission through CET placement only.
  • The student should demonstrate good academic standing and should also have good co-curriculars.
  • The student must demonstrate the financial need with all sources of income of the student. His/her family not to exceed INR 3.00 lakh per annum.
  • Student's immediate family member including his/her brother/sister should not be Government employee/employee and should not be in any government industry/ company/organisation.
  • The student should maintain good discipline with the administration/faculty and staff members of S.I.T.
  • For first semester students, the criteria for selection will be based on the marks scored in the Pre-University Examination.

2)Dr. M.N. Channabasappa Endowment Fund Loan Scholarship

  • Award of loan scholarship is purely based on merit list.
  • Students who are from financially weak backgrounds are provided the Scholarship.
  • Students who are getting financial assistance more than 30% of the total fee from any source will strictly not be eligible for the award of loan scholarship.
  • From the list of top 10 students, only one with the best performance in an interview would get the scholarship.
