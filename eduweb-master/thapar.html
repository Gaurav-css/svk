Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology (TIET):

Founded in 1956 in Patiala, Punjab, Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology (TIET), formerly known as Thapar University, is a private Deemed-to-be University. Top-ranked and innovation-focused private university Thapar University has a "A+" grade from the NAAC. According to the NIRF Ranking 2023, Thapar University is placed 22nd out of all private and government universities in India. According to NIRF 2023, the university is placed 40th overall, 20th in engineering, 49th in management, and 34th in research. Engineering, management, science, and liberal arts are among the specializations in which TIET provides undergraduate, graduate, and doctorate degrees.

The two main programs that Thapar University offers are the B.Tech and MBA. Postgraduate degrees in M.Tech, MA, MCA, and M.Sc. are also offered by the university. Thapar University admits students based on both admission and merit. National entrance examinations like JEE Main, GATE, CAT, MAT, GMAT, NEET, and so on are accepted for admission to TIET. The institution additionally grants admission to different courses in accordance with the results of the most recent qualifying exam.

Thapar University Admission 2024

The Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology accepts applications on the basis of both merit and admission. For admission to a variety of degrees, Thapar University accepts a number of national entrance tests, including JEE Main, NEET, GATE, CAT/GATE/CAT/MAT/XAT/ NMAT/ CMAT/ GMAT, and UGC NET. Applicants who do not have scores at the national level are admitted on the basis of merit or through an entrance exam administered by the university.

Thapar University Placements

Thapar University Placements 2023 is ongoing for different courses.
