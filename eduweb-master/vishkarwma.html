The private Vishwakarma Institute of Information Technology (VIIT) was founded in Pune, Maharashtra, in 2002. Salary packages for the institute's graduates range from INR 5 LPA to INR 15 LPA. Major recruiters such as EQ Technologic, Infosys, Tavisca, Publicis, Sapient, and FINLQ are involved in the VIIT Pune Placements.
The University of Savitribai Phule Pune (SPPU) is associated with the Institute. The Bansilal Ramnath Agarwal Charitable Trust is in charge of the Institute, which was founded as an independent Pune institution. The All India Council of Technical Education and the University Grant Commission have both accredited the institute, which has an A grade and a 3.14 CGPA.

The engineering school Vishwakarma Institute of Information Technology offers undergraduate, graduate, and doctorate programs. Courses listed under "under graduate" include B.Tech and B.Tech (Lateral). M. Tech. courses are examples of post-graduate courses. The Institute also provides Ph.D. programs in a range of engineering specializations.

VIIT Pune Admission 2024

VIIT Pune invites application for admission to the B.Tech programme under 5 specializations. Lateral entry is only available for B. Tech in Civil Engineering. Admission to the course will be based on the marks secured in the either JEE Mains or MHT-CET.

JEE Mains is the national level examination whereas, MHT-CET is a state examination.

Students belonging to Maharashtra state are granted admission on the basis of MHT- CET scores only.

Vishwakarma Institute of Information Technology (VIIT) placement

Vishwakarma Institute of Information Technology (VIIT) is situated at Pune, Maharashtra. VIIT Pune placements 2023 had been initiated and the institute has already engaged with 240 recruiters. Till now, VIIT Pune has recorded a placement of 83.5% and 105 students have been placed. VIIT Pune highest package stood at INR 33 LPA which was offered by Ansys Software. VIIT Pune Facilities

The following facilities are given by VIIT to the students:

Library: There is a Central library in the Institute which consists of latest text books, reference books, hand-books, journals, periodicals, proceedings CDs, etc. It also consists of Reference Section, Journal Section, Reading Hall, Digital library section, Multimedia Facility, Stack-Room etc. Photocopy Services and many more also available in the Library. Book availability search in the library is fully computerized through the SLIM system and can be done from any LAN connected PC on Campus.
Cafeteria: The Institute has 2 Canteens, 1 mess and coffee centre in the Campus which provides healthy food to the students.
Hostel:The Hostel has transport facilities, Solar Heaters, Wi-Fi Facility, Medical Facilities, TV Facilities and many more. There is a separate hostel for Boys and Girls which has at least 100 rooms in it. The rooms have various facilities which provide greater satisfaction to the students.
Labs : There are various labs at VIIT which are Civil Engineering Lab, Chemistry Lab, Computer Lab, Electronics Lab, Language Lab, Mechanical Lab, Physics Lab
Sports Complex: VIIT has various sports provisions such as Chess boards, TT Tables, Playground and many sports are included in the Institute.
Transport: The Institute provides Transport Facilities to the Students so that The Students can be transported easily from their home to the Institute.
